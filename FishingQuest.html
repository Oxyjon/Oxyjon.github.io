Fishing Quest is a 100 Player cozy fishing game set on a curious archipelago.
Embark on a Fishing Quest! Explore 10+ unique islands, reel in rare fish, upgrade your skills, and dive into quests with townsfolk. Team up, compare catches, or conquer global challenges in this ultimate fishing adventure!
