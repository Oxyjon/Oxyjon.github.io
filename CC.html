Closed Captions

Project information

  • Project URL: https://tattooedmilk.itch.io/closed-captions

Play with up to 5 friends in this humorous blast to the past where the audience get to say what goes. Watch a clip from a film and then everyone gets to write their funny caption! Vote for your favourite answers and laugh out loud at the ones that flop.


Note: This Project Uses Photon Networking
