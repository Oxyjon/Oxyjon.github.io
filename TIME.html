T.I.M.E

Project information

  • Category: Game Development
  • Project URL: https://store.steampowered.com/app/2632920/TIME/

T.I.M.E: A ONE VERSUS ONE arena shooter set in a dystopian brutalist interior. Each round changes how you think about your actions! Run-it-back: Sift through previous events and decide where it all went wrong to rectify it next round! Dynamic Pick-Ups: Choose different Power Weapons and Power Ups every round in put in play! Frenetic Movement: Jetpack, Slide and Bounce around to dodge away from, or even toward your enemy!
